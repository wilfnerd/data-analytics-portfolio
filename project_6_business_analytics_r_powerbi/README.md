Sales Performance & Profitability Intelligence Dashboard

Objective

Build an end-to-end business analytics solution to transform raw retail sales data into actionable executive insights using R for data engineering and Power BI for visualization & decision support.

Business Questions Answered

Which regions generate the highest revenue and profit?

How are sales trending over time?

Which customer segments are most valuable?

Who are the most profitable customers?

Which products are driving losses?

How efficient is shipping performance?

Workflow Overview
1. Data Preparation (R)

Performed in R using tidyverse and lubridate:

Cleaned and standardized column names

Parsed and validated date fields

Engineered business metrics:

Profit margin

Order value

Shipping duration

Monthly & yearly trends

Created aggregated customer-level analytics

Exported production-ready datasets to Power BI

Key Outputs

cleaned_data.csv

customer_summary.csv

2. Business Intelligence (Power BI)

Built an interactive executive dashboard featuring:

Core KPIs

Total Sales

Total Profit

Profit Margin

Average Shipping Days

Strategic Visuals

Sales & Profit by Region

Monthly Sales Trend

Segment Performance Comparison

Top 10 Customers by Profit

Loss-Making Product Identification

Key Insights

Certain regions significantly outperform others in profitability

Month-to-month revenue trends reveal seasonality patterns

Corporate and Consumer segments contribute the highest margins

A small group of customers drives a large share of profit

Several products generate consistent financial losses, presenting cost-optimization opportunities

Shipping time efficiency correlates with customer profitability and retention

Business Value

This project demonstrates the ability to:

Translate raw data into business-ready insights

Support executive-level strategic decisions

Identify revenue growth opportunities

Highlight profit leakage risks

Build stakeholder-ready dashboards and reports

Tools Used

R (tidyverse, lubridate) — Data engineering & feature creation

Power BI — Visualization & executive dashboarding

GitHub — Portfolio version control
